One or two sentences on the event’s purpose.

Information like: what attendees need to know to attend this event (prerequisites), exactly what they will make, what they should install in advance, how to get into the building, etc. (Does not include the speakers' bios, if any.)

  • Beginner events don’t require attendees to have prior knowledge about any particular topic.
  • Intermediate events are geared at attendees who may not have any experience with the topic being taught, but know about the background topics it depends on.
  • Advanced events offer a deep dive into a specialized topic and are aimed at grad students or those with outside work or expertise on the subject being taught.
